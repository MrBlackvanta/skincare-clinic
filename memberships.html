Insider FAQs

1.How do I sign up?

Call us at 703-576-8315 or email us at support@thefixclinic.com to sign up.

2.How much does the membership cost?

Fees are either $149 (Elite) or $79 (Access) per month based on membership level. Payment is charged monthly to a credit card of your choice. You can use Visa, MasterCard, Amex, or Discover to sign up.

3.Is there a minimum commitment for memberships?

Yes, there is a 6-month minimum. Memberships are renewed annually but can be canceled after 6 months if you provide a 30-day notice to support@thefixclinic.com.

4.Is there a set-up or initiation fee?

No, there is no set-up or initiation fee.

5.I did not get a chance to use my monthly complimentary treatment. Can I use it the following month?

No, complimentary treatments cannot be carried over or transferred to another person.

6.Can my complimentary treatments or discounts be transferred?

No, monthly services or discounts can only be used by the member. Membership privileges are not transferrable.

7.Membership Cancellation

Memberships can be canceled after 6-months of enrollment by emailing support@thefixclinic.com. A 30-day notice is required.
